FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Have Questions? We've Got Answers

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our courier and logistics services.

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided at the time of booking through our shipment tracking service.

Do you offer international courier services?

Yes. We provide reliable international courier services for documents, parcels and commercial shipments to multiple countries.

Can I schedule a pickup from my home or office?

Yes. Simply contact us by phone or submit a pickup request and our team will arrange parcel collection.

How long will delivery take?

Delivery time depends on the destination and the service selected. Express shipments arrive faster than standard deliveries.

What items can be shipped?

Documents, parcels, business consignments and many other permitted items can be shipped. Certain restricted or prohibited items cannot be accepted.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us by phone, WhatsApp, email or by visiting any of our branch offices during business hours.
